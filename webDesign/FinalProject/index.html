Animal Crossing Community

Visualizing the Animal Crossing Community at time of the big 2.0 update


What are we looking at

It has been reported that Animal Crossing: New Horizons grossed $654 million in global digital sales by the end of 2020, making it the year's seventh highest-grossing digital premium game worldwide. As of March 31, 2021, the game has sold 32.63 million units. (taken from Wikipedia)... talking about how because of the pandemic, the game not only was popular in terms of sales, it also had a very interactive twitter community that helped each other out in building gr eat islands.

With the highly anticipated 2.0 update, we wanted to take a look to see if the already highly active community was reinvigoratted and also explore what they were talking most about!
