What are we looking at?

It has been reported that Animal Crossing: New Horizons grossed $654 million in global digital sales by the end of 2020, making it the year's seventh highest-grossing digital premium game worldwide. As of March 31, 2021, the game has sold 32.63 million units. (taken from Wikipedia)... talking about how because of the pandemic, the game not only was popular in terms of sales, it also had a very interactive twitter community that helped each other out in building great islands.

With the highly anticipated 2.0 update, we wanted to take a look to see if the already highly active community was reinvigoratted and also explore what they were talking most about!

Explaining the community

When examining the community, it is important to not only understand when things are being said or how popular any one statement may be. It is important to look at the community as a whole. To do this, we tapped into the emoji use of the community to get a sense of what else is being used to communicate around the time of the big update. (add insights)
